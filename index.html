My Favorite Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Two cloves of garlic minced
  • Chili paste
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Ib. of chicken cut into small pieces
  • 1 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 4 tbsp of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 cup of kale or spinach
  • 1 cup of rice

Directions:

  1. Cook rice and set aside.
  2. Set pan to medium heat.
  3. Add oil to pan and fry egg, set aside.
  4. Add oil to to pan, add garlic.
  5. Add meat to pan over garlic, stir intermittently.
  6. Once meat is slightly seared on each side, add oyster sauce, soy sauce, and chili paste.
  7. After sauce has been stirred in with meat, add kale or spinach, stir until greens soften.
  8. Serve over fried egg and rice.
  9. Enjoy.
